# FlyBooking POC - Official Airline Policies

## 1. Baggage Allowances
### AirEurope
- **Cabin Baggage**: 1 piece up to 8kg (Dimensions: 55x40x20 cm). Free of charge.
- **Checked Baggage**: Up to 23kg. Costs €45 if booked online, €60 at the airport.
- **Overweight**: Any bag over 23kg incurs an automatic €15 per extra kg fee.

### MedFly
- **Cabin Baggage**: 1 small personal item only (Dimensions: 40x20x25 cm) that must fit under the seat.
- **Checked Baggage**: Up to 20kg. Costs €30 if booked online.

### BritAir
- **Cabin Baggage**: 1 piece up to 10kg (Dimensions: 56x45x25 cm). Free of charge.
- **Checked Baggage**: Up to 23kg. Costs £40 if booked online, £55 at the airport.
- **Overweight**: Any bag over 23kg incurs an automatic £20 per extra kg fee.

### SpanAir
- **Cabin Baggage**: 1 piece up to 10kg (Dimensions: 55x40x20 cm). Free of charge.
- **Checked Baggage**: Up to 20kg. Costs €35 if booked online, €50 at the airport.
- **Overweight**: Any bag over 20kg is charged at €12 per extra kg.

## 2. Cancellations and Modifications
- **Full Refund**: Allowed if the flight is cancelled more than 14 days prior to departure.
- **Partial Refund**: Cancellations between 7 and 14 days before departure incur a 50% penalty fee.
- **No Refund**: Cancellations within 7 days of departure are non-refundable. Only airport taxes will be returned.
- **Modifications**: Date or time modifications are permitted up to 48 hours before departure for a flat fee of €35 plus the fare difference.

## 3. Traveling with Pets
- **In-Cabin**: Small cats and dogs up to 8kg (including their soft carrier) are allowed in the cabin. The fee is €50 per flight.
- **In-Hold**: Pets over 8kg must travel in the ventilated cargo hold. The fee is €120 per flight.
- **Restricted Breeds**: Snub-nosed (brachycephalic) dogs like Pugs and French Bulldogs are strictly prohibited from flying in the cargo hold due to safety regulations.
